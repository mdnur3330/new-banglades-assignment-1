Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the creation and implementation of new ideas, technologies, or methods to solve problems and improve efficiency, driving societal and economic progress.

Sustainability

Sustainability ensures meeting present needs without compromising future generations, focusing on environmental, economic, and social well-being.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure includes the essential physical and digital systems like roads, bridges, and networks that support economic activities and improve quality of life.

Empowerment

Empowerment enables individuals or communities to gain control over their lives, fostering confidence, equality, and opportunities for growth.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Today, I stand before you to share a vision—a vision for a world where poverty is a relic of the past, where every human being has the opportunity to live a life of dignity, and where capitalism serves humanity, not the other way around. For decades, the world has been driven by an economic system that prioritizes profits over people. This has led to extreme inequality, environmental degradation, and a sense of hopelessness for millions. But I believe we have the power to change this. The problem is not with capitalism itself but with how we have structured it. We must rethink the very foundation of our economic systems to ensure that they work for everyone, not just a privileged few.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
